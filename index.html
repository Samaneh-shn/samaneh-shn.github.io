About Me


Obsessed with data, driven by curiosity!

I'm a Data Scientist and HCI Researcher with a strong passion for leveraging data-driven technologies to create interactive systems that positively impact people's lives. My background in computer science, combined with my deep interest in Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), Machine Learning, and Natural Language Processing (NLP), empowers me to approach problems with an interdisciplinary lens. 

I specialize in designing, developing, and evaluating interactive applications and AI-driven tools that enhance user experiences across various fields, including education, healthcare, finance, and sustainability. My projects range from predictive modeling and NLP-based analysis to creating immersive and accessible user interfaces that consider diverse user needs. 

My research and professional practice are driven by the belief that technology should be inclusive and meaningful. I focus on understanding users deeply through qualitative and quantitative methods, designing thoughtful interfaces, developing robust AI systems, and evaluating their real-world effectiveness through rigorous testing. 

Beyond my technical roles, I spent several years teaching English, which enriched my communication skills and reinforced my commitment to accessible and inclusive technology solutions. Currently, I'm exploring new ways to combine interactive systems with AI to create tools that support education, accessibility, and personalized user experiences. 

I'm always eager to connect with collaborators and professionals passionate about making impactful technology solutions. Let's innovate together!
